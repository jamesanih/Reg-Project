# Equipment and Technology Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** February 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** IT Department

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the provisioning, use, maintenance, and return of company-owned equipment and technology resources at Acme Corporation. It ensures employees have the tools needed to be productive while protecting company assets.

## 2. Standard Equipment Packages

### 2.1 All Employees

Every full-time employee receives:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro 14" or Dell XPS 15, based on role and preference)
- External monitor (27" 4K display)
- Keyboard and mouse (wireless)
- Headset with microphone for video calls
- Laptop bag or backpack

### 2.2 Engineering Employees

In addition to the standard package, engineering employees receive:
- Second external monitor
- 32GB RAM laptop upgrade (where applicable)
- Access to cloud development environments

### 2.3 Executive Employees

Director-level and above additionally receive:
- Premium laptop model
- Noise-canceling headphones (Sony WH-1000XM5 or equivalent)
- Mobile hotspot device

## 3. Equipment Request and Provisioning

### 3.1 New Hires

Equipment for new hires is provisioned by IT at least 5 business days before the start date. Managers must submit the new hire equipment request through the IT portal at it.acmecorp.com at least 2 weeks before the start date.

### 3.2 Replacement and Upgrades

- Standard replacement cycle: Every 3 years from date of issue
- Early replacement is permitted for equipment that is damaged, lost, or no longer functional
- Upgrade requests outside the standard cycle require manager and IT director approval
- Request replacements through the IT portal

### 3.3 Additional Equipment

Additional equipment (second monitor, ergonomic accessories, etc.) may be requested through the IT portal with manager approval. Budget considerations may apply; IT will respond to requests within 5 business days.

## 4. Home Office Stipend

### 4.1 One-Time Setup Stipend

New remote-eligible employees receive a one-time $500 home office stipend to purchase:
- Desk and chair
- Lighting
- Cable management
- Other workspace essentials

### 4.2 Annual Technology Stipend

All remote-eligible employees receive an annual $100 stipend for internet-related costs. This is paid as a lump sum in January. Part-time remote employees (hybrid) are also eligible.

### 4.3 Stipend Submission

Home office purchases should be submitted through the expense portal with receipts. Purchases do not require pre-approval if within the stipend amount. Items purchased with company stipends remain company property.

## 5. Equipment Care and Responsibilities

### 5.1 Employee Responsibilities

Employees must:
- Treat company equipment with reasonable care
- Keep equipment clean and in good working condition
- Install software updates and security patches promptly when notified by IT
- Not modify hardware (no opening laptops, no hardware modifications)
- Report lost, stolen, or damaged equipment to IT within 24 hours
- Not lend company equipment to family members or non-employees

### 5.2 Software

- Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices
- A list of pre-approved software is available on the IT portal
- Requests for new software must be submitted to IT for security review
- Personal software (games, personal productivity tools) should not be installed on company devices
- Browser extensions require IT approval before installation

### 5.3 Data on Devices

- Company data should be stored in approved cloud services (Google Drive, company SharePoint), not locally on devices
- Enable full-disk encryption (enforced by IT management tools)
- Do not disable security software (antivirus, endpoint protection)
- Backup critical work to cloud storage regularly

## 6. Equipment Return

### 6.1 When to Return

Equipment must be returned:
- On the last day of employment for voluntary resignations
- Within 5 business days of termination
- When receiving replacement equipment (return old equipment within 5 days)
- When transitioning from remote to on-site work (return duplicate equipment)

### 6.2 Return Process

- On-site employees: Return equipment to the IT desk on the 2nd floor
- Remote employees: IT will send a prepaid shipping box and label. Pack equipment carefully and ship within 5 business days
- A return checklist is provided to ensure all items are accounted for

### 6.3 Failure to Return

Equipment not returned within the specified timeframe will be:
- Reported as lost/stolen
- Remotely wiped and disabled
- The replacement cost may be deducted from the final paycheck (where permitted by law)
- Escalated to Legal for recovery if necessary

## 7. Lost or Stolen Equipment

### 7.1 Immediate Steps

If equipment is lost or stolen:
1. Report to IT immediately at it-support@acmecorp.com or ext. 5000
2. IT will remotely lock and wipe the device
3. File a police report for stolen equipment
4. Submit the police report number to IT
5. HR will assess whether disciplinary action is warranted (negligence cases)

### 7.2 Replacement

Lost or stolen equipment is replaced at no cost for the first occurrence. Repeat incidents within 12 months may result in cost-sharing at the manager's discretion.

## 8. Personal Device Usage (BYOD)

### 8.1 Policy

Acme Corporation does not currently support a Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) policy for primary work devices. However, employees may access company email and Slack on personal mobile devices with the following requirements:
- Device must have a passcode or biometric lock
- Company mobile device management (MDM) software must be installed
- Company reserves the right to remotely wipe company data from personal devices upon separation

### 8.2 Exceptions

BYOD exceptions for laptops or desktops require IT director and CISO approval and are granted only in rare circumstances.

## 9. Equipment Inventory

IT maintains an inventory of all company-owned equipment. Employees may view their assigned equipment in the IT portal. Annual equipment audits are conducted; employees must verify their inventory when requested.

## 10. Contact

For equipment requests, issues, or returns, contact IT Support at it-support@acmecorp.com, ext. 5000, or through the IT portal at it.acmecorp.com. For urgent issues, use the #it-help Slack channel.
