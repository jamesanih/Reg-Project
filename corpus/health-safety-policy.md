# Health and Safety Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 10, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Facilities Department and Human Resources

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes health and safety standards for all Acme Corporation workplaces. The company is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for employees, contractors, and visitors.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all company-owned and leased facilities, including offices, warehouses, and event spaces. It also covers employees working remotely as applicable.

## 3. General Safety Rules

### 3.1 Workplace Conduct

- Keep walkways, exits, and emergency equipment clear at all times
- Report hazards (wet floors, damaged furniture, exposed wires) immediately to facilities@acmecorp.com
- Do not operate equipment you have not been trained on
- Wear appropriate attire for your work environment
- No running in office areas

### 3.2 Visitor Safety

- All visitors must sign in at reception and receive a visitor badge
- Visitors must be escorted by an employee at all times
- Visitors must follow all safety rules while on premises
- In an emergency, the host employee is responsible for guiding their visitor to safety

## 4. Emergency Procedures

### 4.1 Fire Safety

- Fire extinguishers are located at every stairwell entrance and kitchen area
- Fire drills are conducted quarterly; participation is mandatory
- In case of fire: activate the nearest fire alarm pull station, evacuate via the nearest stairwell (do not use elevators), and assemble at the designated rally point in the parking lot
- Floor wardens are trained to assist with evacuation; follow their instructions

### 4.2 Medical Emergencies

- Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies, then call ext. 9000 (Security desk)
- First aid kits are located in each kitchen/break room and at the reception desk
- AED devices are located at the main entrance and on each floor near the elevator
- Trained first responders on each floor are identified by red cross badges

### 4.3 Severe Weather

- In case of tornado warning: move to interior rooms on the lowest floor (designated shelter areas are marked with signs)
- In case of earthquake: drop, cover, and hold on; move away from windows
- Building managers will communicate via the PA system and Slack #emergency channel

### 4.4 Workplace Violence or Active Threat

- If safe, evacuate the building immediately
- If evacuation is not possible, hide in a secure room and lock/barricade the door
- As a last resort, be prepared to defend yourself
- Call 911 when it is safe to do so
- Do not re-enter the building until cleared by law enforcement

## 5. Ergonomics

### 5.1 Office Ergonomics

The company is committed to preventing repetitive strain injuries:
- All workstations are adjustable (chair height, monitor position, keyboard tray)
- Standing desks are available upon request from facilities@acmecorp.com
- Ergonomic assessments are available for any employee by contacting HR
- Employees should take regular breaks: follow the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)

### 5.2 Remote Work Ergonomics

Remote employees should follow the same ergonomic principles:
- Maintain proper posture at your home workstation
- Use the home office stipend to purchase ergonomic equipment
- Request a virtual ergonomic assessment through HR if needed

## 6. Illness Prevention

### 6.1 Sick Employees

Employees with contagious illnesses (flu, COVID-19, etc.) must not come to the office. Work from home if able; otherwise, use PTO. Return to office only after being symptom-free for 24 hours (48 hours for gastrointestinal illness).

### 6.2 Hygiene Practices

- Wash hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
- Hand sanitizer stations are located at all entrances and common areas
- Cover coughs and sneezes with your elbow, not your hands
- Wipe down shared equipment (printers, conference room phones) after use

### 6.3 Pandemic Response

In the event of a pandemic declaration:
- The company will follow CDC and local health authority guidelines
- Remote work may be mandated for all non-essential employees
- Office capacity limits may be implemented
- Enhanced cleaning protocols will be activated
- HR will communicate any changes via email and Slack

## 7. Workplace Wellness Programs

### 7.1 Available Programs

- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Free confidential counseling for personal and work issues. Call 1-800-555-HELP or visit eap.acmecorp.com
- Annual flu shots: Offered free in October at all offices
- Gym membership discount: 30% off at participating gyms (see intranet for list)
- Mental health days: 2 additional paid mental health days per year (separate from PTO)
- Wellness challenges: Quarterly step challenges with prizes

### 7.2 Mental Health Support

The company recognizes the importance of mental health:
- Managers are trained to recognize signs of burnout and stress
- Flexible scheduling is available for employees attending therapy or counseling
- The #wellness Slack channel provides resources and peer support
- Workload concerns should be raised with managers during 1:1 meetings

## 8. Incident Reporting

All workplace injuries, illnesses, and near-misses must be reported within 24 hours to:
- Your direct manager
- Facilities department at facilities@acmecorp.com
- HR department for worker's compensation cases

An incident report form must be completed and submitted to HR within 48 hours. The company will investigate all reported incidents and take corrective action.

## 9. Workers' Compensation

Employees injured on the job are covered by workers' compensation insurance:
- Report injuries immediately to your manager and HR
- Seek medical attention as needed
- HR will provide claim forms and guide you through the process
- The company prohibits retaliation against employees filing workers' compensation claims

## 10. Contact

For safety concerns, contact Facilities at facilities@acmecorp.com or ext. 4000. For wellness programs, contact HR at wellness@acmecorp.com. For emergencies, call ext. 9000 (Security).
